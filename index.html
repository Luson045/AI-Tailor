About AI Tailor

Revolutionizing body measurements with AI and 3D technology

What is AI Tailor?

AI Tailor is an innovative system that generates accurate 3D body meshes from single photographs and extracts precise anthropometric measurements.

Our technology combines state-of-the-art AI models with validated anthropometry standards to deliver professional-grade measurements that rival traditional tailoring methods.

Whether you're in fashion design, healthcare, or ergonomics research, AI Tailor provides the accuracy and convenience you need.
